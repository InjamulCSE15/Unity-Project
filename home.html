Why need Online Exam Hall Seating Arrangement System?

EXAMINATION HALL SEATING ARRANGEMENT SYSTEM is developed for the university to simplify the allocation of halls and issuing to find hall to students during exams. It facilitates to access the examination information of a particular student in a particular department. The information is sorted information alphabetically, which will be provided by the teacher for a respective department. This system is also help in finding the examination eligibility criteria of a student of the particular department.

Some of the advantages of the proposed system are as follows:

  • Develop software such that everybody working in exam hall allocation system can handle easily.
  • Trainer can store & retrieve data easily. And hence, keeping these major target segments in focus, the system was developed.
  • Report can also provided through print outs.
  • Provide a simpler method to store and access information related to exam hall and students.
  • Provide a simple interface which will be easily used without much training.
  • Reduce paperwork and make all related information accessible easily.
